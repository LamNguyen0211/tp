OUR SERVICES

Flatbed Towing

Our services are not limited to delivery of your cargo to any spot on the globe. In the event that your prized car breaks down and needs to be towed to a service station, a flatbed towing truck can provide greater peace of mind that your car will not sustain wear and tear while getting towed to safety. With us, you can request a flatbed tow for your vehicle 24/7.

Many tow truck providers prefer using both a dolly and a flatbed tow truck. The former is inexpensive and agile, so providers often favor it when they need to get a motorist stranded on the highway shoulder. Unfortunately, dolly trucks pass wear and tear onto the car attached, and they cannot be considered reliable enough to deliver your car without any troubles to the nearest repair shop.

Transitec Towing

Traditionally, motorists in need are at the mercy of the nearest tow service provider that is available to come to their aid. They may be able to request a flatbed tow, but this message could get lost in translation from the dispatch to the towing truck. We eliminate middlemen, sending your message straight to our affiliates near your vehicle offering you top-notch towing and delivery services.
