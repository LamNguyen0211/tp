OUR SERVICES

Care Courier

Transitec offers our clients professional courier services within the areas of our clients’ residence. Local knowledge of our couriers is second to none. For example, if your customer/recipient isn't at home but works around the corner, our couriers will usually be happy to make a detour to ensure the successful delivery.

Flatbed Shipping

Our flatbed shipping experts have the tools and relationships to obtain capacity and move your freight safely and on-time. Whether you need regularly contracted flatbed deliveries, a spot shipment or a long haul, we will give you necessary assistance in choosing the most efficient route and the best flatbed trailer for your freight.

Specialized Shipping

If you need more than just an ordinary delivery, this service is just what you need. At Transitec, we will work with you to learn the specific shipping requirements of your freight and help you simplify the transportation process. We monitor your specialized freight shipments to ensure they are delivered to the destination on-time.
